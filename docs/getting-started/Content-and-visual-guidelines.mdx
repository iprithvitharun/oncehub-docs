# Signing Up & Account Creation

Getting started with OnceHub begins with creating your free account.

1. Visit [app.oncehub.com](https://app.oncehub.com)
2. Click **Sign up free**
3. Enter your work email address and choose a secure password
4. Follow the onboarding steps to set up your first booking page or calendar

Once you’re signed up, you'll receive a welcome email and can begin configuring your scheduling tools.

> ✅ Tip: Use a work email if you’re joining a team or an organization.

OnceHub supports direct integrations with popular video conferencing platforms.

### Supported platforms

- Zoom
- Microsoft Teams
- Google Meet
- Webex
- GoToMeeting

### How to connect

1. Go to **Settings > Video Conferencing**
2. Choose your provider
3. Sign in and authorize access

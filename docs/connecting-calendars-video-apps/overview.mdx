---

OnceHub integrates with your calendar and video conferencing tools to automate bookings, avoid conflicts, and generate meeting links automatically.

### Why connect your calendar?

- Sync availability in real-time
- Avoid double bookings
- Automatically block out busy times

### Why connect video tools?

- Auto-generate meeting links for Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Webex, and more
- Include join links in confirmation emails
- Eliminate manual scheduling

You can connect one or more calendars, and OnceHub supports most major platforms.

# Introduction to OnceHub

OnceHub is a scheduling automation platform that helps individuals and teams manage their availability, automate bookings, and connect with leads or customers effortlessly.

Whether you're using it to manage a personal calendar or coordinate a team’s availability across time zones, this guide will help you get up and running.

### What you'll learn

- How to create and manage your account
- How to identify the right product to use
- How to connect calendars and enable notifications
- Where to find important features like the Field Library

Let’s get started!

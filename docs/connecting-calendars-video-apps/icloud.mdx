title: iCloud Integration
---

Using iCloud for your personal or professional calendar? OnceHub lets you connect and sync your availability.

### Steps to connect

1. Create an **app-specific password** in your Apple ID account
2. Go to **Settings > Calendar Integration**
3. Select **iCloud** and enter your credentials

Once connected:
- Your iCloud events will be considered when showing availability
- Bookings will appear in your iCloud calendar

> 🔐 Apple requires app-specific passwords to protect your account. You can revoke access anytime via [appleid.apple.com](https://appleid.apple.com).

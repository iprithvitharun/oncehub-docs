---

Connect your Google Calendar to sync availability, manage events, and auto-block busy times.

### How to connect

1. Go to **Settings > Calendar Integration**
2. Select **Google Calendar**
3. Sign in with your Google account and grant permission

Once connected:
- You can select which calendars to check for availability
- Events created via bookings will appear in your Google Calendar
- Guests receive calendar invites automatically
